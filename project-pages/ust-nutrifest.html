Project Details / Background
This video was commissioned by a client from the Faculty of Medicine in UST ( University of Santo Tomas ). They already have the storyboard and clips ready so I was tasked to compile and edit. Each section was given a theme to comply with and they had the theirs related to Peter Pan. I was given a week to finish this and possibly, copy the editing style of the Netflix show "Iron Chef".
The Netflix-like intro and Iron Chef title card were both created in Adobe After Effects. The editing was compiled and edited in Adobe Premiere for the majority while the addition of closed-captions were added in Final Cut Pro as a last minute request from the client.
