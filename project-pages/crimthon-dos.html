Crimthon 2
A collection of layouts for marketing the event and the event's slides
Project Details / Background
I was tasked to create a layout that would represent our university's Criminal Justice Education department and our office's, UC Innovation and Technology Transfer Office, aesthetic in their joint event. I created various slides with the university's logo and government entities' logo. There's also a slide that doesn't have Lady of Justice in the corner so that the office can have a uniform slide to be used for details. Despite finishing my internship on August, the event was already being planned out before launching it in October of 2022.
The layouts were created in Canva alone where I utilize the application's stock images. The fonts that I advised to be used for titles and subtexts are Open Sans Extra, Poppins Extra Bold, and Squartica 4F while using colors in these hexacodes, #FFCC53 and #013200 (the university's color).
