University of the Cordilleras Promotional Video
An informational and promotional video for our university's alumni homecoming
Project Details / Background
This video was filmed and edited in a span of 2 weeks. I worked on this video with a co-intern where we collaborated with the university's guidance office for guidelines on what the video should include as well as the duration. The university's president also had a say on what we should focus on and gave us access with the actors,facilities, and equipment to be featured and utilized in the video.
The video had a 2 day plan where we utilized MS Word for the storyboard, script, setting, photos, shots, and the transitions that will be used.The video's graphics (location and name of the area) were then created in Adobe Illustrator and the audio narration was recorded in Audacity. Meanwhile, the clips for each department of the university were compiled in Filmora then edited in Final Cut Pro.
